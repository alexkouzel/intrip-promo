Let us do your trip planning

intrip is a search engine that allows users to find desired locations to travel based on their social media.

Product

Intrip is an app that suggests places to visit depending on your interests by analyzing posts on social networks. If you post more mountains, we will recommend fewer nightclubs but more hiking places.
